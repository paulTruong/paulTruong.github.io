About me

Hi, I'm Paul Truong


I'm a web developer with a love for all things web, art and design. I used to work for the Department of Human Services, where I was a developer for the Aged Care payment systems.

Now I build websites for small businesses and individuals, helping them attract more customers and clients. All the while teaching them that the World Wide Web is not as scary or complicated as they thought.

Outside of web development, I also love to play video games, read and dabble in art and design. My favourite artists are Kim Jung Gi and Katsuya Terada, I just love clean linework.

If you are interested in seeing what I can do for you or simply want to chat, please do contact me at mrpaultruong@gmail.com or by filling out this form.
